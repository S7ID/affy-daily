How to read this: "Store sales" = all paid orders that day (net of refunds; wholesale/related-party orders excluded), across every channel. "Blended MER" = total store sales ÷ TOTAL ad spend (Meta + Google + Pinterest) — the single efficiency number for the whole programme. Daily MER swings a lot because ad spend is steady while premium orders arrive in bursts, so the 7-day MER column is the smoother trend. "Meta purch." = orders the Meta pixel attributed directly to an ad click. Google Shopping/PMax went live 22 Jul 2026 and Pinterest (a bridal Consideration test) 24 Jul 2026 — both are in their learning phase, so those numbers build over the first ~2 weeks.
